About Digital Library

Digital libraries are needed both to let libraries offer new and improved services, and to deal with a cost crisis now affecting their current services. Dreams of computer-based libraries, which have been around for decades, are now practical. There are libraries (e.g. in some pharmaceutical companies) which already spend more than half their acquisitions budget on electronic resources, rather than paper. Meanwhile, many university libraries are finding it impossible to maintain their traditional collecting practices within the constraints of their institutional budgets. They look eagerly to the new technology as a solution. The main aim of digital library is to provide the educational resources digitally, so that anyone can access it remotely from anywhere with the help of internet. As of now, it only contains the contains the contents used for engineering or typically in the most common branches of engineering namely-Computer Science Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electricals and Electronics Engineering. This site contains all the neccessary books for each semester used for pursuing these branches, but we will be adding more content very soon.
